Scrambled Eggs

Here is a Recipe for scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold eggs from the edge to the center ever 20 seconds(as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist, remove the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
